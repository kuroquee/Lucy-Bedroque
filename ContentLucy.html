Aesthetics

His work uses 90s and 2000s anime visuals and "Old Web" aesthetics. This includes references to Shibuya Punk (or "Animemo"), a style reminiscent of cel-shaded games like Persona. Bedroque draws influence from the Japanese Visual Kei movement, like the band Malice Mizer.

Bedroque has shown a desire to collaborate with Ecco2k, whose ethereal and fashion-forward approach aligns with his own Visual Kei-influenced style.

Fashion & Style

His style often features flared jeans with few color and punk elements like biker chains and studded accessories. Bedroque often uses androgynous aesthetics, blending masculine streetwear with feminine accents, lace, and flamboyant textures.
